Introduction

Welcome to the Tailored Applications brand guidelines.

This is the system behind how Tailored Applications looks, sounds, and shows up — from a business card to a product screen. It's the brief we hand anyone designing, building, or writing for the brand, so everything feels like it came from one company.

Tailored Applications designs and builds custom digital products for enterprise and growing businesses, concept to launch. The brand carries that same idea: precise and technical, but warm and human — bold without shouting. Made-to-measure, like the apps we ship.

What's ready: the foundations are complete — the logo, color, typography, and voice & tone that hold the brand together. Still in progress: the pattern, illustration style, and photography direction — we're still working on those.

Treat the finished foundations as fixed and the rest as a toolkit. When something isn't spelled out here, return to the spirit: clear, confident, and tailored to fit. Get these right and every touchpoint reinforces the same brand — the consistency that turns a mark into something people recognize and trust.

Always keep clear space around the logo so it stays legible and uncluttered. Maintain a margin equal to the height of the "T" mark on every side, and keep type, imagery, and page edges outside this zone.
